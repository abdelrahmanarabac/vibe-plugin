### 2.1 Gestalt Principles (Visual Perception)
*   **Proximity & Similarity:** Group related features to reduce cognitive scanning.
*   **Figure-Ground & Common Region:** Use depth (Glassmorphism) to isolate active tasks from background context.

### 2.2 Operational Laws
*   **Fitts's Law:** Critical targets (buttons) must be large and positioned high-priority "thumb zones."
*   **Hick's Law:** Minimize choice complexity. Break dense workflows into "Confidence-Aware" steps.
*   **Miller's Law:** Never present more than 5-9 "chunks" of information in a single viewport.
*   **Jakob's Law:** Respect established mental models (e.g., standard Cmd+K omnibox behavior) to minimize the learning curve.
